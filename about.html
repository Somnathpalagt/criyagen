Our Story

Criyagen Agri & Biotech Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2008 with a vision to revolutionize agriculture through scientific innovation. What began as a small enterprise has grown into a leading life science company specializing in agri-input products.

Over the past 15+ years, we have dedicated ourselves to developing comprehensive solutions for crop nutrition and protection, always keeping the farmer's welfare and environmental sustainability at the forefront of our research.
